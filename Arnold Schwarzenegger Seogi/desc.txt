Adds a custom navigation voice pack based off of Arnold Schwarzenegger's voice lines from the Waze app.

Tested on:
- 1.35
- 1.37
- 1.41+ Seogi

This mod should work on later versions. Updates will be released as, and when required. 
Please submit any bugs/feedback on to the relevant Steam Workshop pages.

I give you full permission to modify this mod. 
If you use this mod as part of a mod pack, I'd really appreciate attribution!

Enjoy,
